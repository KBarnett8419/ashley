Meet Chef Ash

Surprise! "The Millenial Cook Book" was actually written by a millenial. Ashley Cox, or affectionately called "Chef Ash," has been cooking scrumptious healthy meals for the past 5 years. As a private chef who learned on the job, she learned that cooking wasn't as hard as she imagined it to be. Her friends and family always complained about never having time to prepare healthy, cost-effective meals so she decide to create a guide to help them out. Simple. Healthy. Delicious. The perfect book for millenials or just people in general.

"The secret to really good food is a sharp knife and untethered imagination."
-Chef Ash
